User Guide Overview

Welcome to the Lagunitas CRM brain-transfer knowledge dump. It’s a heap.

Of information!

We’ve got a lot to cover in a short amount of time, so let’s get right to it. This document will serve as your guide on the journey that is our new CRM (it’s sort of like the light of Eärendil, if you get my meaning). What’s important to remember is that this whole thing isn’t as hard as it looks, and if you’ve used the previous version of the CRM, it’ll be like slipping on a new pair of your favorite shoes. If you’ve never used the CRM before, then congratulations! You just got a new pair of shoes.
Without further a-do, let’s get to it.

Come on in, the water’s warm and the beer is cold!

App Layout

The CRM2 app is broken into 5 sections:

  • On The Call
  • My Stuff
  • Distributor Communication (Dist Commun)
  • Marketing
  • Control Panel

We’ll go through each of these sections in turn. Some of it pretty stock-standard as it was before, while there are some pretty nifty redesigns and overhauls as well.
