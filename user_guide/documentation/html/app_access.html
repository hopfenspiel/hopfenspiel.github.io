App Access(Appcess?)

This section will cover installing and logging into the CRM2 iOS app.

Downloading the New CRM

To download the new CRM app, first open App Updates on your iOS device.

This will lead you to the MobileIron webpage (sort of like our internal appstore), where you will see a link to install the CRM2 app. Pressing this link will install the latest version of the CRM2 app onto your iPad.

Once the install is finished, you will see the (soon-to-be famous) blue CRM2 icon on your device. Nothing to it.

App Login

Logging into the app is as easy as logging into your work computer – in fact, you’ll be using the same username and password! Technology, right?

First, press the blue CRM2 icon to launch the app. This will bring you to the login page.
Use the same username and password as your work computer to log in to CRM2.

After entering your username/password, either press “Login” in the box above or the blue “Go” button on your keyboard. Et voila! C'est Magnifique!
