Stamps come in many shapes and sizes...

...and quite often do not fall into any one category

Here are a few examples of assorted stamps.

The Mauritius Stamp


This stamp was actually created by a Mauritian governor's wife for a ball held at their home and was never actually used in circulation.
There are not many known left in existence.

The Inverted Jenny


This stamp, the Inverted Jenny, is a famous error - or, a misprinted stamp. The plane should be the other way around!

The Penny Black


The Penny Black is not famous for its beauty, but because it's the first ever postage stamp!
