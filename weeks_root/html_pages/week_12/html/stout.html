The Story of Stout

When most people hear the word stout, they think of someone short and stocky in stature. However,
there's also stout beer! When you think about it, the strength and bracing effects of a good stout
may embody the characteristics of someone of the same body type.

Of all the types of stout in the world, there's none more famout than Guinness. Based in Dublin, Ireland,
the first dark beer brewed by Guinness was actually an accident! The brewer at the time accidentally put too much chocolate malt (a type of malted, kilned barley)
into the mash (a part of the brewing process). Although it wasn't intended, the beer proved to be a delight, and was loved so much by porters
that the style of beer - Porter - was named after them!

After porter came a darker version - a stout porter - from which the beer style of stout is born.
